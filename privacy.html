Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

At Chuyển Động Crypto, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle user data in connection with our automated video publishing system integrated with TikTok APIs.

1. Data We Collect

When you authorize with TikTok, we receive limited data such as your TikTok user ID and OAuth2 access token. No additional personal information is collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

Access tokens are used exclusively for uploading and publishing videos. We do not sell, share, or analyze personal data.

3. Data Retention

Tokens are stored temporarily in encrypted form and deleted automatically when expired or revoked.

4. Security

All communications use HTTPS and secure TikTok Open APIs.
